The Journey Begins

How two chance meetings revealed a universal truth about enterprise software

First Meeting

Dr. Renaud - ORL Surgeon, Marseille

It started after a wing foil session at Pointe Rouge in Marseille. Dr. Renaud, an ORL surgeon practicing at two surgery centers including the private Clinic Saint Joseph, shared his frustration. Two years ago, he'd commissioned an Airtable application to manage his patient workflow. "It helped," he said, "but it's not enough. We still have issues with patients not following pre-surgery instructions, and I'm trying to avoid hiring a third secretary for our practice."

"The Airtable app saved us time and money, but I need something more robust. A real platform that manages the entire patient journey - pre and post-surgery - and reduces no-shows and complications from poor instruction compliance."

His Current Challenges:

  • Pre/post-surgery patient workflow management
  • Ensuring patient instruction compliance
  • Coordination between two surgery centers
  • Shared practice with 2 other surgeons, 2 secretaries
  • Avoiding the cost of a third secretary through automation
Four Months Ago (May 2025)

Nicolas Mangin - Serial Entrepreneur

I met Nicolas after a wing foil session in Fos-sur-Mer. A serial entrepreneur who had already started and managed multiple small companies, Nicolas was at a crossroads. He was preparing to sell his public procurement consulting company that works with agglomerations and large cities. "I'm ready for something new," he told me. "I want to build a tech platform that transforms how cities manage public procurement."

"After years of consulting, I know exactly what's broken in public procurement. I want to create a SaaS platform that democratizes access to public contracts and uses AI to level the playing field for smaller companies competing for tenders."

His Vision for the Platform:

  • Automated tender discovery across BOAMP, TED, and local platforms
  • AI-powered bid qualification and matching
  • Compliance automation for Code de la commande publique
  • Collaborative tools for bid preparation
  • Analytics to improve win rates over time
The Revelation

One Platform, Multiple Applications

That night, I couldn't sleep. I kept thinking about both conversations, and then it hit me:

Common Core Needs

  • User authentication & authorization
  • Multi-tenant architecture
  • Document management
  • Workflow automation
  • Compliance tracking
  • Analytics & reporting

Different Business Logic

Medical
  • Patient records
  • Appointment scheduling
  • Medical billing codes
Procurement
  • Tender database
  • Bid preparation
  • Competitor analysis

The answer was clear: Build one robust platform with pluggable business modules. But the challenge remained - how could one developer create all of this?

The Challenge

The Impossible Timeline

Traditional Development Estimate:

Core Platform 3-4 months
Medical Module 4-5 months
Procurement Module 4-5 months
Total Time 11-14 months
Team Required 5-7 developers

The Reality: Both entrepreneurs needed MVPs within 6-8 weeks to secure funding and early customers.

The Discovery

AI-Agent Orchestration

That's when I discovered Claude Code and the concept of AI-agent orchestration. Instead of one developer trying to do everything, what if we had specialized AI agents, each an expert in their domain?

Backend Agent

Expert in Django, databases, APIs

Frontend Agent

Master of React, UX, and interfaces

Security Agent

Guardian of compliance and safety

Tech Lead Agent

Architect and coordinator

The Result: What seemed impossible became achievable. The first working prototype was ready in just 10 days.
